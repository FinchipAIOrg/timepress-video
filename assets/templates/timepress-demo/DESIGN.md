# Timepress Demo Design

## Visual Preset

`xiaohei-sketch`

## 3-second read

Static article illustration goes into a hand-cranked time press and comes out as a playable timeline video.

## Style Prompt

Use a pure white hand-drawn Chinese article-illustration look. This demo uses the optional `xiaohei-sketch` preset: thin black wobbly lines, one deadpan black Xiaohei character doing the core action, restrained orange flow motion, one red warning note, and one blue rendering note. Keep the composition strange, clean, and readable, not like PPT or a formal diagram.

## Colors

- Background: `#ffffff`
- Ink: `#111111`
- Orange flow: `#f57c00`
- Red warning: `#e53935`
- Blue note: `#1e88e5`

## Typography

- Chinese handwritten labels use local system Chinese fonts: `PingFang SC`, `Hiragino Sans GB`, `STKaiti`, sans-serif.
- Labels stay short and large enough for video: 30px to 46px.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use gradients, shadows, paper textures, or dense backgrounds.
- Do not turn the frame into a PPT flowchart or a course slide.
- Do not add a top-left title.
- Do not make Xiaohei decorative in this preset; Xiaohei cranks the machine.
- Do not add more than 6 visible Chinese labels.
